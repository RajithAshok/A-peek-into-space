Kepler 1192b

Kepler-1192 b is an extrasolar planet (exoplanet) that orbits the star Kepler 1192 in the constellation of Lyra. An exoplanet is a planet that orbits a star other than the the Sun. The star is located 3614.00 light years or 1108.03 parsecs from the Earth based on the latest parallax records.

The Orbital Period, or to say it another way, the planets year is 25.2034787 days or 0.07 Earth Years. By the time, the Earth has completed a full year, the planet will have completed 14.48 orbits of its star .
